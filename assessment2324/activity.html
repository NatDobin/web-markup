A Dog's Paradise!

As a proud dog owner in Haarlem, you're in for a treat! Our charming city is not just for humans; it's a paradise for your furry companion too.

Here are some fantastic pet-friendly places and activities to explore!

Remember, while enjoying these pet-friendly places, be respectful of leash laws and always clean up after your dog.

Haarlem is a city that loves its canine residents, so you and your four-legged friend are sure to feel right at home.

Parks and Outdoor Adventures:

Haarlem boasts beautiful parks where your dog can run, play, and socialize.

Don't miss:

Haarlemmerhout: This historic park is perfect for leisurely strolls and picnics with your pup.

Kennemerduinen: Explore the dunes and trails together for a day of adventure.

Here you can find more places to visit.

Dog-Friendly Restaurants and Cafes:

Enjoy a meal or a cup of coffee without leaving your dog at home:

Don't miss:

Café Toussaint: A cozy cafe that's known for its warm atmosphere and dog-friendly policy.

De Wandelaar: A dog-loving restaurant where your pup can relax by your side while you dine.

Many cafes and restaurants in Haarlem welcome four-legged friends.

Pet Shops and Grocery Stores:

Haarlem offers various pet shops where you can find everything your dog needs, from treats to toys. Plus, grocery stores like Albert Heijn often carry pet supplies, making it convenient to stock up.

Pet-Friendly Accommodations:

If you're visiting Haarlem or planning a staycation, there are several pet-friendly hotels and vacation rentals that will happily welcome your dog as their guest.
